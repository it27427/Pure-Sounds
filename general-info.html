Puresounds is a professional online sound library, featuring high-quality sound effects and loops from professional sound designers and cutting edge music producers.

Anyone who needs sound effects can use Puresounds. Our clientele includes everyone from Hollywood movie studios, to community theater directors, to iPhone app creators. Last year, more than 3 million people visited Puresounds from all over the world.

John Doe started Puresounds after a brief career in London's music industry. With a small team of 20 initial contributors, Tasos and a global community of sound enthusiasts grew Puresounds from a small hobbyist site into the professional production resource it is today.

Puresounds is headquartered in Nicosia, Cyprus, but we work with a global team of collaborators all over the world, everywhere from Seattle to London.
