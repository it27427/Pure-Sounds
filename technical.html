First, register for an account. Then purchase a subscription here. You can search for sounds via the search bar at the top, browse by category, or browse by tag. Once you find a sound you like, click the button to the right of the waveform preview to download the WAV file.

To listen to a sound before downloading, click the play button to the left of the waveform. Previewing sounds does not affect your download count.

Puresounds does not allow member uploads. Our library consists of content provided by professional contributors and libraries. If you have sounds you are interested in licensing, send inquiries to admin@puresounds.net.
